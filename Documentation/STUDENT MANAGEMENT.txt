WHY WE DID NOT CREATE RELATIONSHIPS EARLIER

The Student module contains multiple entities.

Instead of creating relationships one by one and generating many migrations,
we first completed every entity.

Now we connect everything once.

Advantages

• Cleaner migrations
• Easier debugging
• Easier ERD review
• Professional EF Core architecture
• Less migration conflicts

ONE TO ONE RELATIONSHIPS

StudentProfile
        │
        ├── StudentPreference
        │
        ├── StudentSettings
        │
        ├── StudentNotificationPreference
        │
        └── StudentSubscription

Reason

These tables extend StudentProfile.

Only ONE record exists for each student.

Example

Student John

StudentPreference
StudentSettings
StudentSubscription

Only one of each.

==========================================================================

ONE TO MANY RELATIONSHIPS

StudentProfile
        │
        ├── StudentAddresses
        ├── StudentDocuments
        ├── StudentEmergencyContacts
        └── StudentWishlists

Reason

A student may have

Many addresses

Many uploaded documents

Many emergency contacts

Many wishlist courses
